About this map

Countries resized to show their total CO2 emissions from 1850 to 2010. These historical (or 'cumulative') emissions are crucial because CO2 can remain in the air for centuries. Europe and the US dominate. The UK, cradle of the industrial revolution, is particularly bloated.
