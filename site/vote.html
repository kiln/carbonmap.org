Alert: World Bank has suspended voting!

The World Bank has suspended voting until April 10 due to errors on their own website. It's very frustrating but there's nothing we can do about it. Thank so much for getting this far – we really appreciate your support – and we hope you'll check back this time next week to vote.

If you'd like a reminder at the time, please follow our new Twitter account or drop a blank email to hello@kiln.it.

If you like this site, please vote for us!

We created this site in our spare time as an entry for the World Bank’s Apps for Climate competition. In addition to a judging process there’s a Popular Choice Prize, which will be awarded to the makers of the app that gets the most votes from members of the public. If you like this site, we’d very much appreciate it if you’d vote for it on the competition website to help our chance of winning. These kinds of competitions don’t always get a huge number of votes, so yours could make all the difference – and it only takes a minute!

How to vote

• Register to vote (30 seconds)
• Click the link in the confirmation email – check your spam filter if you can't see it (10 seconds)
• Once logged in, visit our Carbon Map submission page (5 seconds)
• Give us a good rating! (10 seconds)
