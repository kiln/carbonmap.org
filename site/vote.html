We created this site in our spare time as an entry for the World Bank’s Apps for Climate competition. In addition to a judging process there’s a Popular Choice Prize, which will be awarded to the makers of the app that gets the most votes from members of the public. If you like this site, we’d very much appreciate it if you’d vote for it on the competition website to help our chance of winning. These kinds of competitions don’t always get a huge number of votes, so yours could make all the difference – and it only takes a minute!
