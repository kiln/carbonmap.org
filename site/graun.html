The pay gap between men and women in the same occupation – interactive

Statistics show the wage gap in Australia is at a 20 year high. However the gap varies for different occupations, with some worse and some better than the average. Here you can see the gap over time for various jobs, ranging from labourers to CEOs. Hover over or click the dots to see the specific occupation, and drag or swipe the year text to change years.
